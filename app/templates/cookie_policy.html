Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how the Theatre Web App ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website or use our services. This policy provides you with clear and comprehensive information about the cookies we use and their purpose.

By using the Theatre Web App, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree to the use of cookies, you should adjust your browser settings or stop using the app.
1. What Are Cookies? Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website or use a service. They help the website recognize your device and improve your user experience by remembering your preferences or actions over time. Cookies are commonly used on the web for a variety of reasons, including to: Enable website functionality. Remember login details or preferences. Track website performance and usage patterns. Personalize content and advertisements (if applicable).
2. Types of Cookies We Use We use the following types of cookies on the Theatre Web App: Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the operation of the web app. They include, for example, cookies that enable you to log into secure areas of the app or navigate through the website. Performance Cookies: These cookies allow us to collect information about how users interact with the web app. They help us improve the performance of the app by showing us which pages are most popular, how users move through the site, and if there are any issues with page load times. For example, Google Analytics cookies fall under this category. Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow the app to remember choices you make (such as language preferences or display settings) and provide enhanced features. They help make your experience more personal and enjoyable. Analytics Cookies: We use third-party tools like Google Analytics to collect data on how visitors use the Theatre Web App. These cookies help us understand user behavior, track the effectiveness of our features, and improve our services.
3. How We Use Cookies We use cookies to: Enable basic functionality of the Theatre Web App, such as authentication and session management. Improve the user experience by remembering preferences and settings. Collect usage data to help us analyze trends, enhance our features, and improve the overall performance of the web app. Ensure that the app remains secure and functions correctly.
4. Managing and Controlling Cookies You have the right to decide whether or not to accept cookies. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Most web browsers allow you to: Accept or reject cookies. Set your browser to notify you when a cookie is being set. Delete existing cookies from your device. Please note that if you disable cookies, some parts of the Theatre Web App may not work properly, and your experience may be less personalized. For instructions on how to manage cookies in your browser, you can visit the help section of your browser or use the following links for popular browsers: Google Chrome Mozilla Firefox Safari Microsoft Edge
5. Third-Party Cookies The Theatre Web App may contain third-party cookies, such as those used for analytics or social media integration (e.g., Google Analytics). We do not control these third-party cookies, and they are governed by the privacy policies of the respective third parties. For more information on third-party cookies used by the Theatre Web App, we encourage you to review the privacy and cookie policies of the third-party services we use.
6. Changes to This Cookie Policy We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will post the updated policy on this page and revise the "Effective Date" at the top of the page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically for any updates.
7. Contact Us If you have any questions or concerns about our use of cookies or this Cookie Policy, please feel free to contact us.
